How to Use the Twitter Previewer

01

Compose Your Tweet

Type your tweet text. The live counter tracks your 280-character limit in real time so you never get cut off.

02

Set Your Profile

Enter your display name, handle, and verification badge so the preview matches your actual Twitter/X profile.

Professional Twitter Planning

Twitter's 280-character limit means every word counts. The difference between a tweet that drives clicks and one that gets ignored often comes down to how the first two lines read in the feed. Our Twitter / X Post Preview Tool lets you visualize your tweet inside an authentic dark-mode X UI before publishing — no app required.


Whether you're a social media manager scheduling content for a brand, an entrepreneur announcing a product launch, or a creator growing an audience, this tool helps you catch formatting issues before they go live. Hashtags and mentions are highlighted exactly as they appear on the platform, so you can ensure your calls to action land correctly.


The image preview renders in the native 16:9 Twitter card format, so you can verify your photo isn't cropped in an awkward place. Combine this tool with our Instagram Preview and LinkedIn Preview tools to plan your entire cross-platform content strategy before you publish anything.
