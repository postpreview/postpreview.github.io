How to use the Facebook Previewer

1. Enter Brand Details

Input your Facebook Page name to see how your branding matches the content.

2. Compose Your Message

Type your post text. Pay attention to how the first few lines appear before the "See More" cut-off.

3. Add Visuals

Upload a JPG or PNG to test aspect ratios and see if your image text is readable in the feed.

Why use a Facebook Mockup?

Facebook's algorithm prioritizes content that looks professional and engages users. By using a Facebook Post Preview tool, you ensure that:

  • Important information isn't cropped out of images.
  • Your hashtags and links are positioned for maximum clicks.
  • Your brand voice looks consistent in the dark mode/standard UI.
