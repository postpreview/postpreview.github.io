💡 Twitter/X Pro Tips

URLs are always counted as 23 characters regardless of their actual length. Images, videos, and GIFs don't count toward your character limit. X Premium subscribers get 25,000 character long-form posts.

Writing Within Character Limits: Expert Tips

✂️
Front-load your hook
Put your most compelling line first. Most platforms truncate captions after 125–477 characters, so make those first words count.
🔗
Use URL shorteners
On Twitter/X, all URLs count as 23 characters automatically. On other platforms, short URLs save characters and look cleaner.
📝
Write long, then edit down
Write everything you want to say first, then cut ruthlessly. Every word should earn its place. Aim for 80% of the character limit.
🧵
Use threads for long content
Twitter threads, LinkedIn articles, and Facebook Notes let you go deeper without squeezing everything into one post.
👁
Preview before publishing
Use PostPreview to see exactly how your caption will be truncated on each platform before you hit post. Saves time and embarrassment.
🎯
One message, one post
The tighter the character limit, the more focused your message needs to be. Trying to say two things in 280 characters never works well.
