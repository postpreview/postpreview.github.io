Which Platform Should You Choose?

The answer depends entirely on your audience, content type, and goals. Here's a simple decision framework:

🎯 Quick Decision Framework

Selling products to consumers? → Instagram + TikTok
B2B or professional services? → LinkedIn first, then Twitter/X
Building a community? → Facebook Groups + YouTube
Targeting Gen Z? → TikTok is non-negotiable
Long-form expertise content? → YouTube + LinkedIn
Real-time news & conversation? → Twitter/X
