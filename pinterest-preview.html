Why use a Pinterest Mockup?

Pinterest is a visual discovery engine. Unlike other feeds, Pinterest uses a multi-column staggered layout. Using a Pinterest Pin Preview tool ensures:

  • Your text overlays on images aren't cut off by the feed UI.
  • Your Pin titles are punchy enough to lead with the most important keywords.
  • The vertical aspect ratio (2:3) is optimized for mobile scrolling.

Optimize for Discovery

Pinterest is about intent. By previewing your Pin, you can see if your imagery immediately communicates the value of your link. High-performing Pins usually feature clear typography, high-contrast images, and a strong brand logo.

How to use the Pinterest Previewer

1. Upload Your Creative

Select a vertical image. Pinterest recommends a 2:3 aspect ratio (e.g., 1000 x 1500 pixels) for best performance.

2. Craft a Keyword-Rich Title

Type your title. Only the first 30-40 characters usually show in the main feed, so put your hook first!

3. Set Your Brand Identity

Add your account name to see how your profile avatar and branding look alongside the Pin creative.

Pro Tip: Safe Zones

Avoid placing critical text in the bottom right corner of your image creative, as Pinterest sometimes overlays icons like the "Visual Search" tool in that area.
