What is the TikTok Preview Tool?

The TikTok Preview Tool is a powerful simulator designed for creators, social media managers, and brands who want to perfect their "For You" page (FYP) presence. TikTok's interface is unique, with text overlays and action buttons covering significant portions of your video.

Our tool helps you ensure that critical visual elements, text overlays, and captions aren't hidden behind the TikTok UI, allowing for maximum engagement and clear communication of your message.

Why Use a TikTok Mockup?

  • Avoid "Safe Zone" errors where text is covered by buttons.
  • Test how your caption hook reads in the first two lines.
  • See how your profile picture and username blend with your content.
  • Optimize your hashtags for visual clarity.
  • Perfect your aesthetic before you hit publish.

How to Use the TikTok Previewer

  • Step 1: Enter your TikTok handle in the Username field to see how it appears over the video.
  • Step 2: Type your caption and hashtags. Pay attention to where the text breaks‚Äîthis is where the "more" button usually appears.
  • Step 3: Upload a screenshot or thumbnail of your video to see it in the vertical 9:16 aspect ratio.
  • Step 4: Adjust the Sound Name to match your brand or trending audio choice.
  • Step 5: Review the live preview on the right to ensure your content is in the "Safe Zone."

TikTok Optimization Tips

Keep your most important text overlays in the center-top area. Avoid the bottom 25% and the right-side 15% of the screen, as these are "danger zones" where the caption and side buttons (Like, Comment, Share) will block your content.

Captions on TikTok are now longer, but only the first few words are visible in the feed. Use our tool to make sure your primary hook is seen immediately.
